Paris

Paris is famous for its cafe culture, the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, Notre Dame, the Moulin Rouge, pastries, and fashion. Paris is often seen as having beauty, elegance, and as being a boldly romantic city.

Berlin

Noted for its cultural flair, Berlin is home to the world famous Berlin Opera and Berlin Philharmonic Orchestra, while its diverse art scene encompasses hundreds of galleries, events, and museums, including those on Museum Island, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Amsterdam

Amsterdam, the capital of the Netherlands, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Europe. Amsterdam is famous for its canals, beautiful houses, museums, Art, 'coffee' shops and Red Light District, but there's plenty more to this liberal city than most visitors realise. .

Vienna

Vienna offers a unique blend of imperial traditions and stunning modern architecture. It is famous for its cultural events, imperial sights, coffee houses, cozy wine taverns, and the very special Viennese charm..
